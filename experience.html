My Journey in Finance & Data

My path into finance and data science has been shaped by hands-on experience across investment management, real estate finance, and student leadership. I believe the best decisions are made when rigorous quantitative analysis meets clear communication — and that's the standard I hold myself to in every role.

At MFS Investment Management, I've learned how institutional asset managers think about markets, evaluate active strategies, and communicate investment insights to clients. Working with Bloomberg, FactSet, and eVestment has given me a deep appreciation for how data drives every layer of the investment process — from portfolio construction to client reporting.

On the academic side, my coursework in machine learning and database systems has pushed me to think computationally about problems I encounter in finance. I've built classification models, designed relational databases, and created data visualizations that tell stories with numbers. I'm always looking for ways to bridge the gap between technical tools and business outcomes.
